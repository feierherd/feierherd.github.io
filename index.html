I am a PhD candidate in the Political Science Department at Yale University. My main interest lies at the intersection of comparative politics and political economy, with a focus on economic informality, distributive politics, and political behavior. I am also interested in methods for causal inference in observational and experimental studies. My dissertation studies informality in labor markets, with a focus in Argentina and Brazil.
