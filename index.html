I am a PhD candidate in the Political Science Department at Yale University. My main interest lies at the intersection of comparative politics and political economy, on phenomena that affect developing countries: state capacity, the informal economy, and income inequality. My dissertation studies informality in labor markets, with a focus in Argentina and Brazil.
